Beauty of a Heart
Root yourself in kindness deep, And let your inner beauty bloom; For while the physical fades to sleep, A pure heart lights the darkest room. Trust the path and stay sincere, In grace, your truest worth is found; Like winter snow, both soft and clear, Where peace and beatitude abound.
Chasing the Moon
Pursuing the moon, where secrets reside, She teaches us lessons the daylight might hide. Though scarred and imperfect, her elegance glows, A luminous beauty that everyone knows. She dwells in the darkness to guide us through night, Inspiring the world with her borrowed white light. Not always full, but forever she’s true— Be the moon in a sky full of stars for someone new.
Midnight
Some nights are meant for reflection, In the quiet of lonely hours; But darkness cannot drive out the dark, Only light has the healing power. Though dusk may feel cold and lonely, And Luna stands solo in flight, Few know the secret of shadows: How beautiful the dark is at night. A sky full of stars and a whisper, Can mend every wound with their grace; For there’s magic hidden in midnight, That the sun could never replace.
Sunset’s Grace
In this view, the sunset brings Proof of the beauty ending brings. Whatever blooms for us to see, One day will fade with history. This final truth will always be: All that begins must cease to be.
The Shooting Star
Lost in its light, I forgot my wish, Entranced by a trail of stardust bliss. A bewitching glow that sparks a fire, Connecting the heart to hope and desire. In the deep of night, let the lesson be: To wish for self-love, and set yourself free.
